Problem Statement

  • Overpriced Medicines: Brand-name drugs inflate costs by 200-500%
  • Financial Burden: 60% of healthcare expenses are out-of-pocket
  • Trust Deficit: Generics are available but lack public trust & visibility
  • Confusion: People don't know which generics are legitimate or where to safely purchase them
"The average Indian family spends ₹2,500+ monthly on medicines alone"
MediRam | 2

Opportunity

  • Massive Market: ₹50,000+ crore annual medicine market in India
  • Digital Transformation: Rising smartphone and internet penetration
  • Policy Support: Government actively promoting generics through Jan Aushadhi
  • Consumer Readiness: Customers willing to switch if trust, quality, and price are ensured
MediRam | 3

Our Solution

"Same Medicine. Trusted. Cheaper."
  • Verified Quality: Only DCGI-approved, batch-tested medicines
  • Omnichannel Presence: User-friendly mobile app, website, and strategic physical stores
  • Smart Technology: AI assistant to suggest alternatives to expensive medications
  • Trust Building: Complete transparency on sourcing, testing, and equivalence
