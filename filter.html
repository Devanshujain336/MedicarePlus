Health Insurance Schemes

1. Ayushman Bharat Pradhan Mantri Jan Arogya Yojana (AB PM-JAY)

Income: Up to ₹15,000 All India All Categories
Coverage: Health insurance coverage of ₹5 lakh per family per year for secondary and tertiary care hospitalization.
Eligibility: Poor and vulnerable families identified based on SECC database; approximately 10.74 crore families (50 crore people).
Benefits: Covers 3 days of pre-hospitalization and 15 days of post-hospitalization expenses, 1,400+ procedures, all pre-existing conditions.
Application: PMJAY Golden Card can be obtained at PM-JAY empaneled hospitals or Common Service Centers with Aadhaar card, ration card, or other specified ID.
More Info:https://beneficiary.nha.gov.in/

2. Central Government Health Scheme (CGHS)

All India Government Employees
Coverage: Comprehensive medical care to central government employees, pensioners, and their dependents.
Eligibility: All central government employees, pensioners, members of Parliament, ex-MPs, freedom fighters, and their dependent family members.
Benefits: OPD consultation, medicines, lab tests, imaging, hospitalization, specialized treatments at empaneled private hospitals.
Application: Submit application through parent department with prescribed forms, ID proof, and photos.
More Info:https://cghs.gov.in/

3. Employees' State Insurance Scheme (ESIS)

Income: Up to ₹21,000 All India All Categories
Coverage: Self-financing health insurance and social security scheme for Indian workers.
Eligibility: Employees earning up to ₹21,000 per month in organizations with 10+ employees (20+ in some states).
Benefits: Medical, sickness, maternity, disability, and dependent benefits; super-specialty treatment.
Application: Employer registers employees; employee contribution is 0.75% of wages, employer contributes 3.25%.
More Info:https://www.esic.gov.in/

4. Maharashtra's Mahatma Jyotiba Phule Jan Arogya Yojana (MJPJAY)

Income: Up to ₹1,50,000 Maharashtra All Categories
Coverage: Health insurance coverage of ₹1.5 lakh per family per year.
Eligibility: Families with yellow/orange ration cards, farmers in 14 suicide-prone districts, and all residents of tribal districts.
Benefits: Cashless treatment for 996 surgeries/therapies/procedures through 492 empaneled hospitals.
Application: Apply at empaneled hospitals with ration card, income certificate, and Aadhaar.
More Info:https://www.jeevandayee.gov.in/

5. Tamil Nadu Chief Minister's Comprehensive Health Insurance Scheme

Income: Up to ₹72,000 annually Tamil Nadu All Categories
Coverage: Health insurance coverage up to ₹5 lakh per family per year.
Eligibility: Families with annual income less than ₹72,000 and all senior citizens.
Benefits: Covers 1,200+ procedures, implants, and diagnostics.
Application: Apply through e-seva centers or government hospitals with income certificate, Aadhaar, and family card.
More Info:https://www.cmchistn.com/

6. Biju Swasthya Kalyan Yojana (BSKY)

Odisha All Categories
Coverage: Annual health coverage of ₹5 lakh per family (₹10 lakh for women).
Eligibility: All residents of Odisha with priority for economically vulnerable families.
Benefits: Cashless healthcare at empaneled public and private hospitals.
Application: Apply at Mo Seva Kendras with Aadhaar, ration card, and proof of Odisha residency.
More Info:https://bsky.odisha.gov.in/
Maternal & Child Health Programs

1. Pradhan Mantri Matru Vandana Yojana (PMMVY)

All India All Categories
Benefit: Cash incentive of ₹5,000 in three installments for first living child.
Eligibility: Pregnant women and lactating mothers, excluding those who receive similar benefits under other schemes.
Process: First installment (₹1,000) at early registration, second (₹2,000) at 6 months with at least 1 ANC, third (₹2,000) after childbirth registration and first vaccination.
Application: Apply at Anganwadi centers or health facilities with Aadhaar card, bank account, and ID proof.
More Info:https://pmmvy.wcd.gov.in/

7. Chiranjeevi Yojana

Income: BPL & Lower Income Gujarat All Categories
Benefit: Free institutional delivery services through empaneled private providers.
Eligibility: BPL and lower-income pregnant women in Gujarat.
Services: Normal deliveries, C-sections, and management of obstetric complications.
Application: Register at local health center with BPL card and Aadhaar.
More Info:https://gujhealth.gujarat.gov.in/

8. KCR Kit (Telangana)

Telangana All Categories
Benefit: ₹12,000 for a boy child and ₹13,000 for a girl child, plus a kit of 16 essential items for newborn.
Eligibility: All pregnant women who deliver in government hospitals in Telangana.
Kit Contents: Baby soaps, oils, powder, cradle, clothes, mosquito net, toys, diapers, etc.
Application: Automatic enrollment upon delivery at government facility.
More Info:https://www.telangana.gov.in/
Rural Healthcare Initiatives

1. National Rural Health Mission (NRHM)

All India (Rural) All Categories
Coverage: Comprehensive primary healthcare in rural areas.
Facilities: Sub-Centers, Primary Health Centers (PHCs), Community Health Centers (CHCs).
Services: Maternal and child health, immunization, disease control, primary care, emergency care.
Implementation: Through ASHAs (Accredited Social Health Activists) and ANMs (Auxiliary Nurse Midwives).
More Info:https://nhm.gov.in/

2. Ayushman Bharat - Health and Wellness Centers

All India All Categories
Coverage: Comprehensive primary healthcare through 1.5 lakh Health & Wellness Centers.
Services: 12 essential health services including NCDs, mental health, elderly care, palliative care, eye care, ENT.
Benefits: Free medicines, diagnostics, teleconsultation.
Access: Visit nearest Health & Wellness Center in your area.
More Info:https://ab-hwc.nhp.gov.in/

3. Mobile Medical Units (MMUs)

All India (Remote Areas) All Categories
Coverage: Healthcare services to remote, inaccessible areas through mobile vans.
Services: OPD consultation, basic lab tests, medicines, ANC/PNC, immunization, health education.
Frequency: Most MMUs visit villages on a fixed schedule (weekly/monthly).
Access: Check with local ASHA worker or PHC for MMU schedule in your area.
